Solar Eclipse 2015: A UK Weather Map

An interactive map showing UK weather during the solar eclipse of March 20th


Inspired by the University of Reading's genius National Eclipse Weather Experiment citizen science project, EDINA put together this interactive map to show weather changes in the UK during the solar eclipse on 20th March 2015. The map shows reports from the MET Office's excellent DataPoint Observations feed which provides hourly updates from 175 weather stations across the UK.
